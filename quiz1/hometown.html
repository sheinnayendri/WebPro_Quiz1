SURABAYA


Battle of Surabaya Sura and Baya Monument

From this giant header, you all should have known that my hometown is Surabaya.
Well maybe some of you already guessed it based on this website background.
Anyway, Surabaya is one of the most beautiful city located in East Java, Indonesia.
Eventhough it's quite crowded and hot, but the ambience itself is still quite nice and enjoyable.
Surabaya is also known as the City of Heroes, because of the tragedy on November 10th, 1945,
called Battle of Surabaya, where we the Pemuda fought against the British troops in Hotel Yamato,
and one of the Indonesian militia tore off the blue part of the Dutch flag, changing it into the Indonesian flag.
Aside from this heroic battle, Surabaya is also known of its Sura and Baya monument, which represents
the legend of Surabaya itself (this website background is Sura and Baya monument).
I think that's all I can say about my lovely hometown, Surabaya.
